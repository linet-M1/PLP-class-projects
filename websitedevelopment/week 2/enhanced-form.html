Featured Recipe: Classic Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • Optional toppings:
    • Fresh strawberries
    • Maple syrup
    • Whipped cream

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk milk, egg, and butter in another bowl.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  4. Pour batter onto a heated pan.
  5. Cook until golden on both sides.
  6. Serve warm with syrup or fruits.
