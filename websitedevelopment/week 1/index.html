This is a short reflection on how I got started with tech. It's my first step in learning web development and applying HTML5 concepts.

How I Got Started

My interest in tech began with curiosity — wondering how websites and apps work behind the scenes. I joined PLP Academy to gain the skills and guidance I needed.

Challenges I Faced

At first, terms like "semantic HTML" or "syntax" felt intimidating. But by breaking them down, learning step by step, attending lectures and encouragement from peers and instructors, I've gained confidence.

Seeing others well of in their journey compared to me beginer level is overwhelming. comparable to an ant climbing Mt. everest.

What I've Learned

  • Start simple. Focus on structure.
  • Everyone's journey is different. Staying true to myself goes a very long way.
  • its not going to be easy, but in the end it will all be worth it.
